A fragrant, mild, and slightly sweet curry known for its vibrant yellow color and aromatic spices

Ingredients

  • 8 Chicken pieces (thighs and drumsticks)
  • 3 Tablespoons of Cape Malay curry powder
  • 1 Teaspoon tumeric
  • 2 Onions, finely sliced
  • 3 Potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 Tablespoon apricot jam (the secret ingredient)

Steps

  1. Fry the onions until translucent and golden.
  2. Add the spices and toast them for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken and brown slightly, coating every piece in the spice mix.
  4. Add potatoes and a splash of water, then simmer for 35-40 minutes.
  5. Stir in the apricot jam at the end and serve with yellow rice and sambals.
