Hearty Slow-Cooked Oxtail

Ingredients

  • 1.5kg Oxtail pieces
  • 2 Large onions, chopped
  • 4 Carrots sliced
  • 2 Cups beef stock
  • 1 Cup dry wine
  • 2 Tablespoons tomato paste
  • Fresh thyme and bay leaves

Steps

  1. Brown the oxtail pieces in a heavy pot until golden, then set aside.
  2. Saute onions and carrots until soft.
  3. Stir in tomato paste and herbs, then return the meat to the pot.
  4. Add wine and stock, cover, and simmer on low for 3-4 hours.
  5. Serve with creamy mashed potatoes or dombolo(dumplings).
